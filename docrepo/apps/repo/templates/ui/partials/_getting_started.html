Customizing DocrepoX dashboard_customize

DocrepoX has three levels of customization:

  1. Configuration: Involves adjusting configuration variables, usually without the need to write any code.
  2. Extending: Requires extending templates and modules. Documendous Software provides structured ways to override or subclass components of its products.
  3. Deep customizations: Involves changing applications and modules within the apps directory. For guidance, refer to the “Contributing to Documendous” section.
